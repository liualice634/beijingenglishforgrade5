Unit3 Lesson10 Flowers in Our Life 课文跟读

Robot：Hello! Welcome to our Flower World.
Sara：Thank you! Can you tell me something about flowers?
Robot：Yes. Flowers are a part of plants. They can do a lot for you.
Sara：What can flowers do for us?
Robot：Flowers are beautiful. They can clean the air and make you feel happy.
Sara：Yes. What else can flowers do?
Robot：They can give you food and drink, like flower cakes and flower tea.
Sara：That's right. I like flower cakes and my mum likes flower tea. Is there something else?
Robot：People give flowers to show their love and care.
Sara：Yes. I give flowers to my mum on Mother's Day and to my teachers on Teachers' Day.
Robot：You are a clever girl!!
