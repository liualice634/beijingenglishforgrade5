Unit6 Lesson21 Work for My Dream Job 课文跟读

Hello, I am Taotao. I was born in Heilongjiang. In winter, it's very cold and there is ice and snow everywhere, so people like skating there. When I was five years old, my father took me skating for the first time. I always fell over at the beginning, but I enjoyed skating from then on.

I'd like to be a skater when I grow up. I have a big dream. I wish to join the Winter Olympics one day. I'm doing my best to make my dream come true. I do lots of exercise every day. I run for 30 minutes every morning. I take skating lessons every weekend.

"Where there is a will, there is a way." I will work hard for my dream job and never give up.
