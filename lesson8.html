Unit2 Lesson8 The Growth of a Bean Seed 课文跟读

Hello, I'm a bean seed. How can I grow into a strong plant? Let me tell you.

First, I need some soil and water. They are important to me. With their help, I will begin a new life.

Then, I will try my best to grow out of the soil. Wow, what a wonderful world! I need sunlight, air and water to make food.

Little by little, my stem grows taller and taller, and I need a stick to stand up.

After a few days, I begin to flower. I'm so beautiful. Bees dance around me. They can help me grow fruit.

When my flowers fall, some little bean pods will come out. In the pods, there are my new seeds.

This is how I grow into a plant. Thank you all for helping me.
