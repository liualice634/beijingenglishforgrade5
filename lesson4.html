Unit1 Lesson4 Face Problems Bravely 课文跟读

One day, Xiaoran had a big problem. What happened? She broke Chenchen's favourite pen at school. Xiaoran was afraid to face Chenchen. She told Mum about it.

Mum：You should face it bravely.


The next day, Xiaoran and Mum bought a new pen. They went to Chenchen's home together.

Xiaoran：I'm so sorry I broke your favourite pen. Here is a new one for you.
Chenchen：That's all right, Xiaoran.


They became good friends again.

Xiaoran and Mum came back home, but Xiaoran couldn't find her cat, Dudu. Xiaoran forgot to close the window. Maybe Dudu ran away. Xiaoran was worried. She looked for him everywhere.

Xiaoran got an idea. She put up a notice with Dudu's picture and Mum's phone number. Luckily, Dudu came back by himself. Xiaoran was happy again.

So if we face problems bravely, all of them will go away.
