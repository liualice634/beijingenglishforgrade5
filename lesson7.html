Unit2 Lesson7 Travelling Seeds 课文跟读

Seeds have no feet, but they can travel to many places. How do they do that? Look, seeds can travel in many ways.

Some seeds travel with wind. They are small and light, and the wind can carry them easily to other places.

Some seeds travel with water. The plants live by the sea, so their seeds may fall into the sea, and the water will take them away.

Some seeds travel with people and animals. They may stick to people or animals. Animals also eat fruit and carry the seeds to different places.

Some seeds travel by themselves. They have thick coats. The coats will break open when the seeds grow.

How amazing! Do you think seeds are good travellers?
