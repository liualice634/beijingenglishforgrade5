Unit6 Lesson22 My Hero - Yuan Longping 课文跟读

Yuan Longping was a famous Chinese scientist. In 2019, he got the Medal of the Republic, the highest honour in our country. People around the world call him the "father of hybrid rice".

Yuan Longping was born in 1930. When he was young, many people had no food to eat. He felt sad for that, and decided to solve the problem. He worked hard for his dream. He once said, "I'm a farmer. If I am not at home, I must be on the farmland. If I am not on the farmland, I must be on my way to the farmland." In the 1970s, Yuan Longping and his team invented hybrid rice. This kind of rice has a higher output, and can feed more people each year.

Yuan Longping worked on hybrid rice for over 50 years. His research has helped a lot of people in China and other countries. He is my hero.
