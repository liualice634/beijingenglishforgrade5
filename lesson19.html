Unit6 Lesson19 Different Jobs 课文跟读

Mum：What are you reading, Mike?
Mike：I'm reading a book about jobs.
Mum：Sounds interesting. What jobs do you know about from the book?
Mike：I know about doctor, nurse, worker, cook, pilot, astronaut and many other jobs.
Mum：What jobs are you interested in?
Mike：I am interested in being a pilot. Pilots can fly a plane in the beautiful sky.

Dad：Come on, Baobao. Let's watch the rocket launching.
Baobao：That's exciting. What do astronauts do?
Dad：They take a spaceship and fly to space.
Baobao：Look at that woman astronaut. What will she do in space?
Dad：She will do many things. She will pilot the spaceship, and do a lot of research in space.
Baobao：Great. When I grow up, I'd like to be an astronaut, too.
