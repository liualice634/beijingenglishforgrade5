Unit1 Lesson1 Keep Your Things Tidy 课文跟读

Baobao：Mum, did you see my Chinese book?
Mum：I saw it on your desk last night.
Baobao：But it isn't here.
Mum：Oh, what a mess!
Baobao：Sorry. I was too busy yesterday. I had no time to tidy up my room.
Mum：You should put everything in order. Then it will be easy to find your books.
Baobao：OK. Let me tidy them up.

Dad：Look, whose books are these?
Baobao：Oh, they are my books. This is my Chinese book. Thank you, Dad. Where did you find them?
Dad：They were on the floor in the living room. You should keep your things tidy.
Baobao：OK, Dad. I will try my best.

一、动词过去式
see (原形) → saw (过去式) v. 看见
is (原形) → was (过去式) v. 是
are (原形) → were (过去式) v. 是（复数）
find (原形) → found (过去式) v. 找到
二、整理相关词汇
tidy adj. 整洁的；v. 整理
tidy up 整理，收拾（短语）
mess n. 杂乱，混乱
what a mess 真是一团糟！（感叹句）
in order 整齐，有序
三、时间与地点
last night 昨晚
yesterday n./adv. 昨天
desk n. 书桌
floor n. 地板
living room n. 客厅
四、其他重点词汇
Chinese book 语文书
busy adj. 忙碌的
whose pron. 谁的
try my best 尽我最大努力（短语）
should modal v. 应该
