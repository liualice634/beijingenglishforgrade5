Unit6 Lesson20 My Dream Job 课文跟读

Mike：What is your dream job, Maomao?
Maomao：I want to be a writer. Writing is my dream job.
Mike：I'm sure you can make it. You are good at reading and writing.
Maomao：Thanks. But I still have a lot to learn. What's your dream job?
Mike：I'd like to be a pilot in the future. I want to fly a plane in the sky.

Lingling：What do you want to be in the future, Sara?
Sara：I want to be a doctor, because I want to help sick people. What's your dream job?
Lingling：My dream job is to be a teacher.
Sara：Why do you want to be a teacher?
Lingling：Because my mum is a teacher. She is kind and she knows a lot. She always helps her students. I'd like to be a teacher like her.
