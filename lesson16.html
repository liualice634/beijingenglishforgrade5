Unit5 Lesson16 Shop in a Clothing Store 课文跟读

Sara：Wow, so many shirts here! Mum, my shirts are small. I'd like to have a new one.
Mum：They are in so many different colours. Which colour would you like?
Sara：The blue and the red both look nice. They would go well with my black skirt.
Mum：You can try them on.
Sara：Mum, the red one is better.
Mum：Great. What else would you like?
Sara：I'd also like a pair of shoes for my PE class.
Mum：OK. How about this pair? Size 36. They are your size.
Sara：Let me try them on. They are comfortable.
Mum：I will take the shirt and this pair of shoes. How much are they?
Sales girl：You can get 80 yuan off for two. They are 340 yuan now.
Mum：Oh! Thanks.
