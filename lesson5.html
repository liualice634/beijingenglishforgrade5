Unit2 Lesson5 Different Parts of Plants 课文跟读

Guide：Welcome to our garden. What do you know about plants?
Sara：In spring, plants grow fast. It's green everywhere.
Baobao：Plants are one part of nature.
Sara：Grass, trees and flowers are all plants. We can see them everywhere.
Guide：Yes, you are right.

Guide：Look, a plant has different parts. What are their names?
Sara：They are roots, stems, leaves, flowers, fruit and seeds.
Baobao：Do all plants have these parts?
Guide：No, only flowering plants have all of these parts.
Sara：Oh, I see. What do the different parts of plants do?
Guide：They have different roles. Please come with me and have a look.

一、植物的各个部分
plant n. 植物；v. 种植
root (单数) → roots (复数) n. 根
stem (单数) → stems (复数) n. 茎
leaf (单数) → leaves (复数) n. 叶子
flower n. 花
fruit n. 果实
seed (单数) → seeds (复数) n. 种子
二、植物类型
grass n. 草
tree n. 树
flowering plant 开花植物
三、生长与自然
grow v. 生长
spring n. 春天
nature n. 自然
garden n. 花园
四、其他重点词汇
different adj. 不同的
part n. 部分
role n. 角色，作用
have a look 看一看（短语）
everywhere adv. 到处
guide n. 导游，向导
